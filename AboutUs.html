    • 📜 Company History – Krazy Dealer

    Founded: 2021
    Headquarters: Lucknow, India
    Founders: Shevendu & Team

    Krazy Dealer was born from a simple yet bold idea — to make online shopping fun, affordable, and unforgettable. What started in 2021 as a quirky online storefront with just a handful of trending products quickly captured the hearts of budget-savvy, deal-hunting customers across India.

    In a digital world dominated by corporate giants, Krazy Dealer carved its niche by offering a crazy-good mix of value, personality, and customer care. The brand’s smiling red shopping bag mascot became a symbol of joy, surprise discounts, and playful online experiences.

    🚀 Key Milestones

    • 2021: Launched the first Krazy Dealer online store with 50+ handpicked products.
    • 2022: Introduced limited-time “Krazy Deals” — flash sales with viral success.
    • 2023: Expanded nationwide shipping and added a mobile-friendly platform.
    • 2024: Hit 1 million orders and launched our custom merchandise line.
    • 2025: Introduced AI-powered recommendations and same-day delivery in select cities.

    🎯 Our Mission

    To become India’s most fun-tastic shopping destination where quality meets quirk — with deals so good, they’re krazy.

    🌟 Our Vision

    To redefine how people shop online — turning every visit into an adventure filled with excitement, humor, and irresistible prices.
